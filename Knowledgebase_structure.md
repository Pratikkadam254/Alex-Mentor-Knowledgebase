## Key Sections Explained

### 01 Strategy & Foundations
The core "Hormozi Operating System" books. **Start here.**
- `$100M Offers` - How to create offers so good people feel stupid saying no
- `$100M Leads` - How to get strangers to want to buy your stuff
- `$100M Money Models` - The math behind the business

### 02 Scaling Roadmap
Step-by-step guides for each stage of business growth.
- Stage 0: Foundations (before you start)
- Stage 1-8: Progressive scaling from $0 to $100M+

### 03 Departmental Playbooks
Tactical "How-To" documents organized by business function.
- **Sales**: Closing scripts, objection handling
- **Marketing**: Ads, cold outreach, hooks, proof
- **Operations**: Retention, LTV, scaling systems
- **Branding**: Reputation building, content
- **Pricing**: Psychology, raising prices

### 04 Raw Intelligence
Unrefined data for mining insights.
- Podcast transcripts
- Social media archives
- Raw audiobooks

### 99 Archive
Legacy files kept for safety but not part of core workflow.
